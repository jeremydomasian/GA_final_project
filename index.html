Who we are.

Christian Dona

Film and Photography

When Christian is not behind the camera, shooting a short, his passion is in his video editing. Whether it's performing a J/L cut, matching on cut or even deciding which footage should be left on the cutting room floor, Christian's attention to detail gives him the upper hand when editing meticulous raw footage.

Elvin Fontanilla

Literary and Visual Art

Besides rapping on the mic and acting in short films, Elvin spends all his time drawing tattoos for clients. His tattoo illustration styles vary from traditional, tribal as well as contemporary. He is currently on his way to an apprenticeship at a local tattoo parlor in San Francisco.

Jeremy Domasian

Web Development and Design

Jeremy is currently in an internship with a start-up company named Splunk. However, when pre-production for a short comes up, Jeremy is the one in charge with the creative process. Filling plot holes, smoothing out continuity and making sure the project is cohesive, are just a few of his jobs on set
